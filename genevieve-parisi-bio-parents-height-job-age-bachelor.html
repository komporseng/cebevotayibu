Meet Bachelor's Genevieve Parisi! Her Bio, Parents, Height, Age, Job

The drama around the upcoming season of the Bachelor has already begun. The first episodes have not yet aired as of Dec 2021. And apparently, the showrunners have already revealed the identity of the top three. Genevieve Parisi is one of the ladies who have been speculated as one of the top three.

Continue reading the article to know more about the whole story of the spoiler. Also, get to know who Genevieve is.

Genevieve Parisi On Bachelor 

On 6 December 2021, ABC released a teaser for the new season of The Bachelor with Clayton Echard as the leading man. And apparently, the teaser was full of spoilers and even revealed the top three ladies of the season.

In the teaser, Clayton was saying to two ladies that he was “intimate with both of them”. As a result, The Bachelor fandom came to believe that the two women in the teaser were two of the top three finalists. TVInsider believed that Gabby Windey, Rachel Recchia, and Genevieve are the three finalists.

Reality Steve thought the two women in the video were Rachel Recchia and Sarah Hamrick. And that the incident happened before Clayton let Sarah go from the show. The other two finalists, according to Reality Steve, were Gabby Windey and Susie Evans.

Previously, Reality Steve had predicted that Rachel, Gabby, Susie, and Serene Russel were the top four. That means Genevieve was eliminated out of the top four. The reports are conflicting and it is not confirmed whether Genevieve goes to the top three or is eliminated out of the top four.

To say a little about the Bachelor himself, Clayton is a former football player from Eureka, Missouri. Clayton played football for Missouri Tigers. Later, he was signed to Seattle Seahawks. However, he never made the final roster and he retired from professional football in 2019.

Currently, he is working as a sales representative in Columbia, Missouri. He is 28 years of age.

Interestingly, during an interview with GMA, Clayton revealed that he found love during this season of Bachelor. The identities of the top three might still be not anything more than speculations. But one thing is certain, the new season of The Bachelor has been a success.

Genevieve Parisi Parents And Siblings

Genevieve Parisi is the daughter of parents Anne Lopes Parisi and Kenneth Parisi. Not much was known about her father, Kenneth. But her mother, Anne was a nurse and worked for Lifespan. She went to Salve Regina University and studied Nursing. She is from New Bedford, Massachusetts. Her parents have been married for 32 years and are still together.

Genevieve has a “twin” sister called Nicole Parisi. Nicole currently lives in New York. She went to Elon University in North Carolina.

Genevieve got her name from her maternal grandmother Genevieve Lopes Rochefort. Grandma Genevieve died in 2015 at the age of 73.

Trivia: One should pronounce Genevieve’s name “JE-nuh-VEEV puh-REE-see”.
