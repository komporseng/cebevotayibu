Biography

Arturo Muselli was born in Naples, Italy on June 3, 1983. Italian actor who is known for his roles both in feature films and in television shows. He is recognized for his recurring role as Enzo in the 2017 series Gomorrah. He is also known for his roles in the feature films Tenderness in 2017 and La Parrucchiera in 2017 as well. He starred alongside Marco D’Amore and Salvatore Esposito in the 2014 television series Gomorrah. He started his career as an actor in 2004 when he made his debut as Titta’s son in the feature film The Consequences of Love. He made his television debut in 2008 in the series La Nuova Squadra before landing his first recurring role in the 2015 series The Teacher where he played Matteo Maffei.
