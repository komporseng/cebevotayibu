Biography

Tom Gores was born in Nazareth, Israel on July 31, 1964. American businessman who became a billionaire as the founder of the hugely successful private equity firm Platinum Equity. In 2011, he purchased the NBA’s Detroit Pistons for $325 million. When he took over the Pistons organization, he kept Joe Dumars as the President of Basketball Operations until 2014. He moved from Israel to Genesee, Michigan in 1968. He played football, baseball and basketball at Genesee High School.
