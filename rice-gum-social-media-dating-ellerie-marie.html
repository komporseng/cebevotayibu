RiceGum On Social Media Platforms

RiceGum falls under the group of young people carrying out the career as a social media personality with substantial followers. He is widely known for streaming gaming videos on Twitch as well as on his YouTube channel, where he posts several updates about him and his professional life.

He is also a singer with some of his original songs, including Its Every Night Sis, Frick Da Police, DaAdult, My Ex, Bitcoin, and many others, which you can find on his official YouTube channel.

RiceGum On Social Media Platforms

RiceGum is all over the social media platforms. Not only that, but he has managed to garner millions of fans and followers on the platforms. His YouTube channel is the most followed social media platform, which has more than 10.3 million subscribers. The channel was created back on 25 September 2012 and has received over 2,146,235,361 views, as of this writing.
