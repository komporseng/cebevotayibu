Yūsuke Murata (Japanese: 村田 雄介 MURATA Yūsuke; born July 4 1978)[1] is a mangaka, and artist on the Pokémon series. His work was featured in a collaboration known as Pokémon EX Drawing, where he created illustrations for a series of merchandise. He is best known for his adaptation of One Punch Man, originally by ONE.
